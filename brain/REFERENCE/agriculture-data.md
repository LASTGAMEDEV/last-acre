## 🐛 Common Pests & Diseases (Mediterranean)

### Pests
- **Olive fly** — olive fruit damage
- **Corn borer** — stalk damage
- **Aphids** — general crop pest
- **Red spider mite** — greenhouse crops
- **Mediterranean fruit fly** — fruit damage

### Diseases
- **Powdery mildew** — grapes, cucurbits
- **Downy mildew** — grapes, potatoes
- **Fusarium wilt** — many crops
- **Olive knot** — olive trees
- **Blight** — tomatoes, potatoes

## 💰 EU Agricultural Policy (CAP)

- Direct payments based on hectares
- Greening requirements (crop diversification, permanent grassland, ecological focus areas)
- Young farmer scheme (extra support for under 40)

---

## 📊 Spanish Agricultural Stats (Approximate)

- Spain is EU's largest olive oil producer
- Major exporter of wine, fruits, vegetables
- Irrigated agriculture concentrated in southeast
- Dryland farming (rainfed) common in interior
