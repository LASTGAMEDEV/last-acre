🤠 AI Competitor Farmers

Named rival farmers who buy land, plant crops, and compete for parcels. They can outbid you at auctions and expand over time.

📈 Land Value Fluctuations

Parcel prices shift based on surrounding activity — a competitor buying nearby raises prices. Being first in a region keeps it cheap.

C

🏔️ Terrain & Regions

The map is divided into named regions (River Valley, Highlands, Plains). Each region has a climate flavor that favors certain crops.

D

🔗 Adjacency Bonuses

Owning neighboring parcels gives a small yield or efficiency bonus — rewards consolidating your farm into a contiguous block.

E

🏗️ Infrastructure Visible on Map

Roads, silos, and water sources shown as map icons. Distance from a road affects transport cost.

F

⚠️ Event Zones

Drought, flood, or pest outbreaks affect a region of the map — not just one parcel. You can see the spreading zone before it hits you.

G

🌫️ Fog of War / Scouting

Distant regions start hidden. Buy a scout report to reveal what's available, or build a watchtower to see further.

H

🚛 Distance to Market

Fields far from town cost more to transport. Build roads or buy nearby to cut transport costs shown on the map.
