More Map Feature Ideas

Round 2 — pick anything that fits your vision

I

🦠 Disease & Pest Spread

Blight or pests spread from parcel to parcel across the map visually — if your neighbor's field is infected, yours is at risk. Creates urgency and spatial awareness.

J

🍂 Seasonal Map Visuals

The map changes appearance with the season — snow in winter, golden in harvest, fresh green in spring. Makes the passage of time feel alive.

K

🏛️ Landmarks & Special Tiles

Fixed map features — a river, an old mill, the Market Town. Parcels adjacent to them get unique bonuses. Makes certain land genuinely more valuable.

L

🔓 Expansion Zones

The map starts small. New regions (Highlands, River Delta) unlock as your farm grows past milestones. Gives the feeling of a world opening up.

M

🔍 Competitor Intel

By default you can't see what crops competitors are growing — only their owned land. Pay for a scout report to reveal their planted crops and harvest schedule.

N

💧 Water Rights

River-adjacent parcels have natural irrigation access (cheaper to irrigate). Parcels far from water need wells or expensive infrastructure.

O

⏱️ Live Auction Countdown

Premium parcels show a live countdown on the map cell when they go to auction. Competitors bid in real-time (simulated). Creates pressure to act fast.

P

🌍 Soil Type Visible on Map

A toggle layer shows the soil type of each parcel — loamy, sandy, clay, chalky. Lets you plan which crops suit which region before buying.
