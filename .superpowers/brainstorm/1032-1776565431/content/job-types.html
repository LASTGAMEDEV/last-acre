Worker Job Types

15 roles across 5 departments — applicants may arrive pre-certified or not

Role Dept What They Do Possible Pre-Certs
🌾 Fields
Field Hand Fields Planting, weeding, manual harvesting, irrigation checks. Most common hire. Junior workers, rarely pre-certified. Pesticide Handler
Agronomist Fields Soil analysis, crop rotation planning, fertiliser programs, yield forecasting. Raises requests for major interventions. Senior role. Certified Agronomist Soil Science Diploma Pesticide Applicator
Crop Scout Fields Daily field walks to catch pests, disease, water stress early. Reports findings, triggers consultant/vet requests. Eyes on the ground. Pest ID Certificate IPM Practitioner
Irrigation Technician Fields Manages water schedules, drip/sprinkler systems, aquifer draw. Requests approval for major water use. Maintains water infrastructure. Water Management Cert Irrigation Systems License
🐄 Animals
Livestock Hand Animals Daily feeding, watering, mucking out, basic health checks. Flags anything unusual to the vet. Entry-level animal worker. Animal Welfare Cert
Veterinarian Animals Diagnoses illness, prescribes treatment, performs procedures. Always pre-certified. Requests approval for expensive treatments. Veterinary Degree ✦ Livestock Med License
AI Inseminator Animals Artificial insemination specialist. Manages breeding schedules, semen selection, pregnancy checks. High-value genetics work. AI Technician License ✦ Reproductive Science
Farrier / Hoof Tech Animals Hoof trimming and care for cattle, horses, pigs. Prevents lameness. Seasonal demand spikes. Can be hired per-visit or full-time. Registered Farrier ✦
🚜 Machinery
Tractor Operator Machinery Operates tractors and attached implements — ploughing, planting, spraying, baling. Requires machinery license for large vehicles. Tractor License Sprayer Cert
Combine / Harvester Op Machinery Operates combine harvesters, grain carts, forage harvesters. High seasonal demand. Often comes from contracting firms. Combine License ✦ Grain Handling Cert
Farm Mechanic Machinery Maintains and repairs all machinery. Reduces breakdown frequency. Flags machines needing replacement. Saves money long-term. Agricultural Mechanics Cert Welding License
🏭 Processing
Processing Technician Processing Operates processing equipment (grain drying, dairy, meat prep). Maintains quality standards. Requests approval for large batches. Food Safety Cert HACCP Trained
Quality Controller Processing Tests outputs for contamination, grade, moisture, fat content. Can reject batches. Unlocks premium market access if rating stays high. Food Quality Inspector Lab Testing Cert
🚛 Transport
Transport Driver Transport Moves product to market, auction, or processing. Drives farm trucks and livestock trailers. Required for time-sensitive deliveries. HGV License Livestock Transport Cert ADR (Hazmat)
Pre-certification rule
Certs marked ✦ are always held by that role (e.g. a vet always has their degree). All others appear randomly — an applicant might have them, might not. Senior/experienced applicants are more likely to arrive certified.
