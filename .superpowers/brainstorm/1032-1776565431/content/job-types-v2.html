Worker Job Types — Revised

Crop Scout merged into Agronomist · Factories require minimum headcount · New roles suggested below

Role Dept What They Do Possible Pre-Certs
🌾 Fields
Field Hand Fields Planting, weeding, manual harvesting, irrigation checks. Entry-level, rarely pre-certified. Pesticide Handler
Agronomist + Crop Scout Fields Daily field walks for pests/disease/stress AND soil analysis, crop rotation, fertiliser programs, yield forecasting. Triggers pest/disease requests. Senior role. Certified Agronomist Soil Science Diploma Pesticide Applicator IPM Practitioner
Irrigation Technician Fields Manages water schedules, drip/sprinkler systems, aquifer draw. Requests approval for major water use. Water Management Cert Irrigation Systems License
🐄 Animals
Livestock Hand Animals Daily feeding, watering, mucking out, basic health checks. Flags anything unusual to the vet. Animal Welfare Cert
Veterinarian Animals Diagnoses illness, prescribes treatments, performs procedures. Requests approval for expensive interventions. Always pre-certified. Veterinary Degree ✦ Livestock Med License
AI Inseminator Animals Artificial insemination, breeding schedules, semen selection, pregnancy checks. High-value genetics work. AI Technician License ✦ Reproductive Science
Farrier / Hoof Tech Animals Hoof trimming and care. Prevents lameness. Can be hired per-visit or full-time. Registered Farrier ✦
🚜 Machinery
Tractor Operator Machinery Operates tractors and implements — ploughing, planting, spraying, baling. Tractor License Sprayer Cert
Combine Operator Machinery Operates combine harvesters, grain carts, forage harvesters. High seasonal demand. Combine License ✦ Grain Handling Cert
Farm Mechanic Machinery Maintains and repairs all machinery. Reduces breakdowns. Flags machines needing replacement. Agricultural Mechanics Cert Welding License
🏭 Processing
⚙️ Minimum headcount rule: Each factory/processing building has a required worker count. Below minimum = factory paused. At minimum = runs at reduced efficiency. Full staff = full output.
Processing Technician Processing Operates processing equipment. Maintains quality standards. Requests approval for large batches. Counts toward factory minimum. Food Safety Cert HACCP Trained
Quality Controller Processing Tests outputs for contamination, grade, moisture, fat. Can reject batches. Unlocks premium market access. Counts toward factory minimum. Food Quality Inspector Lab Testing Cert
🚛 Transport
Transport Driver Transport Moves product to market, auction, or processing. Required for time-sensitive deliveries. HGV License Livestock Transport Cert ADR (Hazmat)
🏢 Office NEW DEPT?
Farm Administrator NEW Office Handles paperwork, subsidy applications, insurance claims, government compliance forms. Without one, you do all admin yourself — missing deadlines costs money. Agricultural Admin Cert Accounting Diploma
Security Guard NEW Office Reduces theft events (machinery, livestock, fuel). Bigger farms = higher theft risk without security. Night shift option. SIA License First Aid
Department Foreman NEW Office Supervises a chosen department. Reduces the number of permission requests that reach you (they handle the routine calls). One foreman per department max. Farm Management Cert
Pre-certification rule
Certs marked ✦ are always held by that role. All others appear randomly — experienced applicants more likely to arrive certified.
