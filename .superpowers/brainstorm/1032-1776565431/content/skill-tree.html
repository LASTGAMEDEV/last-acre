Key rules:
• XP gained from daily work — no grinding, just time on the job
• Certifications require study time (worker is less productive while studying) + an exam fee
• Branching at Tier 3 — each role has 2–3 specialisation paths, worker can only pick one
• Pre-hired workers may arrive with Tier 1–2 nodes already unlocked depending on their years of experience
• Master level = full autonomy — they never need your approval for their domain
