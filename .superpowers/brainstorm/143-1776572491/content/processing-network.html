Key interconnections:
• Abattoir → feeds Butchery, Charcuterie, Smokehouse, Tannery, Rendering Plant simultaneously
• Dairy → Cheese Dairy produces whey byproduct → Whey Processing → animal feed
• Oil Press → Refined Oil Plant → Confectionery Plant / Bakery / Margarine
• Sugar Refinery → Confectionery + Jam Kitchen + Ice Cream + Brewery
• Rendering Plant → Wax & Soap Factory → Premium Cosmetics
• Biogas Plant (electricity spec) → uses manure, also ties into Compost Plant
• Biofuel Plant → output replaces farm fuel purchases
• Feed Mill → reduces animal feed costs across all livestock
• Biochar Kiln → output improves soil drainage (ties into NPK spec)
