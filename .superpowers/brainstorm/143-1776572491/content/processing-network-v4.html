Farm Shop Processing Network

The rule: Every product must pass this test — "Could a farm make this with the right equipment and sell it at their own shop?"  ·  Think Clarkson's Farm Shop, artisan dairy farms, small-batch breweries, lavender farms.
Scale: Artisan / small-batch. Not industrial. Equipment you could fit in a barn or outbuilding.

← barley, rye, wheat

🌽 Feed Mixer
← grain + soy + alfalfa
→ compound animal feed (sell to other farms)
