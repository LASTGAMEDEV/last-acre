Where does the new content live in the UI?

Your current tab structure needs homes for: Character/Life, Family Tree, History/News, and Family Management. Pick the approach that feels right.

A

New top-level "Dynasty" tab

Add one new tab alongside Farm, Market, Office, etc. — the Dynasty tab contains sub-tabs for: Character (your farmer's profile, health, skills), Family (spouse, children, their roles/skills), Tree (family tree across generations), and Chronicle (historical news feed of events that fired). Everything dynasty-related is in one place.

Pros

  • Clean separation from farm ops
  • Obvious home for all new content
  • Easy to extend

Cons

  • One more top-level tab to navigate
B

Expand existing "Office" tab

Office already handles admin/business. Add new sub-tabs inside it: Character, Family, Legacy (family tree), Chronicle (news). The office becomes the hub for both business and personal/dynasty management — it makes sense thematically (the farmer's personal office is where family photos and records live).

Pros

  • No new top-level tabs
  • Thematically coherent

Cons

  • Office gets crowded
  • Dynasty stuff buried inside Office
C

Persistent HUD + dedicated screens

Add a persistent "farmer portrait + year + age" strip at the top of every screen. Tapping it opens the Character screen. Family Tree and Chronicle live as their own full screens accessed from the main menu or a new "Life" tab. Historical events surface as a notification banner that slides in over any screen.

Pros

  • Year/age always visible = constant historical immersion
  • Events feel like breaking news, not buried in a tab

Cons

  • HUD changes affect every screen
  • Most complex to build
