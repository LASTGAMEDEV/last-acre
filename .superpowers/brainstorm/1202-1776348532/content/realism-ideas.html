What would make Granja Tycoon more realistic?

Pick any ideas that interest you — we'll dig into the best ones together

💧 Water & Irrigation
A

Water Network System

Wells, reservoirs, pipes. Water is a resource you must manage — drought drains your reserves, rain refills them. Different crops draw different amounts daily.

B

Water Rights & Costs

Buy water rights for your region. Drought triggers rationing — you choose which fields to sacrifice. Groundwater pumping costs money and degrades the aquifer over time.

🌿 Crops & Soil
C

Pest & Disease Cycles

Seasonal insects and fungal outbreaks. Scout fields, apply treatments, build resistance through rotation. Mismanage and an infestation spreads across your farm.

D

Rotation Planner

Visual multi-year field plan. Legumes restore nitrogen, cereals deplete it. Monoculture gets progressively penalised; smart rotation compounds bonuses.

E

Organic Certification

3-year transition period with yield penalty but +40–80% price premium at the end. Compliance checks. Lose certification if you spray during transition.

🐄 Animals & Livestock
F

Veterinary System

Scheduled vaccinations, health inspections, disease outbreaks. Neglect vet care and animals get sick. Hire a farm vet to reduce outbreak risk.

G

Feed Management

Animals need specific feed rations. Grow your own fodder or buy it. Feed quality affects milk/meat/egg yields. Winter storage planning matters.

H

Herd Demographics

Calves, heifers, breeding cows, culls — manage the herd lifecycle. Older cows produce more milk but need more vet care. Optimal herd age distribution gives best economics.

⚙️ Equipment & Operations
I

Maintenance Schedules

Service intervals (every X hours of use). Skip service → breakdown risk during harvest. Emergency repairs cost 3× as much. Hire a mechanic or do it yourself.

J

Fuel & Operating Costs

Each machine burns fuel per job. Fuel price fluctuates with oil market. Bulk storage tank cuts cost. Biogas from your own animals can offset diesel.

💰 Farm Economics
K

Government Subsidies

Apply for area payments, agri-environment schemes, investment grants. Eligibility depends on farm size, practices, certifications. Bureaucracy takes time.

L

Cooperative Membership

Join a farming co-op for bulk input discounts and guaranteed floor prices. Pay annual dues. Vote on co-op policy. High-rep members get better terms.

M

Carbon Credits

Earn credits through cover crops, reduced tillage, hedgerow planting. Sell on carbon market for bonus income. Ties realism to sustainability mechanics.

🌍 Environment & Community
N

Environmental Impact

Over-fertilisation causes river runoff, triggering fines and rep loss. Slurry management matters. Build buffer zones. Go beyond compliance for eco grants.

O

Land Value Dynamics

Land appreciates with soil health and region prosperity. Your farm's reputation raises nearby plot prices. Speculators can outbid you for prime parcels.
