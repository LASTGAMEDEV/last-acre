🔑 Key Rules

• Till required before plant — parcel must be tilled to accept a crop
• Harvest requires combine — no combine = hire contractor or crop waits
• One job per tractor — busy tractor can't be reassigned
• Attachment size match — small fits small+medium tractor; large fits large only
• Pickup/Semi need a trailer to transport — no trailer = no cargo capacity
• Dump Truck is standalone transport — no trailer needed, transport only
• Contractor = instant — costs $/ha, no machine required
• No truck = -12% sell price — contractor cut built into price
